Clinical Variant Calling Pipelines (Nextflow)

nf-core/sarek, Parabricks, & Custom Workflows for Clinical Diagnostics

Production Clinical-Grade

Implemented and validated multiple Nextflow-based variant calling workflows including nf-core/sarek, NVIDIA Parabricks-accelerated pipelines, and custom clinical workflows. These pipelines perform comprehensive germline and somatic variant calling with multi-caller consensus approach, achieving 99.7% accuracy validated against Sanger sequencing. Fully automated from FASTQ to annotated VCF with clinical interpretation.

Technology Stack

Pipeline Frameworks: Nextflow DSL2, nf-core/sarek v3.4, NVIDIA Parabricks
Alignment: BWA-MEM2, Parabricks fq2bam, SAMtools, Picard
Germline Calling: GATK HaplotypeCaller, Strelka2, DeepVariant, FreeBayes
Somatic Calling: Mutect2, Strelka2, Manta, CNVkit, TIDE
Annotation & Filtering: GATK VQSR, SnpEff, VEP, HardFiltering
QC & Reporting: MultiQC, BCFTools, Custom Python/R scripts

Bacterial Genome Assembly Pipeline (nf-core/bacaas)

High-Quality De Novo Assembly & Annotation Workflow

Production Validated

Deployed nf-core/bacaas pipeline for automated bacterial genome assembly from raw sequencing reads. Workflow includes quality control, assembly, polishing, quality assessment, and comprehensive functional annotation. Achieved average N50 >100kb and 99.2% genome completeness (BUSCO score) across 200+ bacterial isolates.
